How It Works

This is not a simple add-to-cart store. CB Kitchen is a quote-based, custom sourcing network built for professional contractors.

01

Join the Private Network

Apply for membership and gain access to our exclusive contractor sourcing platform.

02

Submit Your Project Details

Share your project specs, finishes, quantities, and timeline with our team.

03

Receive a Custom Quote

We source options from our verified manufacturing partners and deliver a tailored quote.

04

Production & Quality Review

Your order enters production with quality checkpoints throughout the manufacturing process.

05

Shipping & Delivery Coordination

We coordinate logistics from factory to your job site, with tracking and support at every stage.

Quality Assurance

We don't just source products — we build verified, long-term partnerships with manufacturers who meet our standards.

Personal Supplier Inspections

We travel internationally to inspect suppliers, tour factories, and verify production capabilities before establishing any partnership.

Verified Quality Standards

Every supplier is vetted for material quality, construction methods, and finish consistency before we build long-term partnerships.

Premium Materials & Packaging

We focus on premium wood, hardware, finishes, and protective packaging to ensure products arrive in perfect condition.

Reduced Delays & Damage

Our processes are designed to minimize production delays, shipping damage, and quality inconsistencies.

Long-Term Contractor Relationships

We're not interested in one-time sales. Our goal is building lasting partnerships with contractors who return project after project.
